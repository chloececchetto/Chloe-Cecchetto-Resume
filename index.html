Lifegaurd

Lifegaurd, CPR, First Aid, and Emergency Oxygen Administration Certified

Over my years as a swim coach and camp counselor I came to fully appreciate the amount lifeguards do not just to keep patrons safe, but also to keep the pool running. I've maintained my CPR and first aid certifications for the last 4 years and this winter I completed my lifeguarding and emergency oxygen administration certifications. I understand each aquatic facility has its own tailored set of rules and protocols to keep patrons safe, and am ready to learn and adapt to a new team and its needs. As a coach and counselor I learned how to work as a team with my coworkers, communicate quickly and effectively in emergencies, and manage large groups in the water while ensuring their safety.

I enjoy working with others, as Captain of both my Highschool and Club swimteams I was involved in the orginizing of team bonding events.
